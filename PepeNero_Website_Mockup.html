Our Story

Italy, in the heart of the African bush.

You did not fly to Botswana expecting to find handmade pasta and wood-fired flavors. But here we are, on President Avenue in Kasane, serving the kind of Italian food that takes you straight back to a kitchen in Rome.

Every dish is made with fresh ingredients, real technique, and the care that only comes from someone who grew up cooking this way. We just happen to be 80 kilometers from Victoria Falls.
